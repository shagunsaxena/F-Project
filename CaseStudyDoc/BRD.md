**Executive Summary**

The objective of this project is to design and develop a premium, interactive product microsite for the launch of the iPhone 17 Pro. The platform combines immersive storytelling with seamless purchasing functionality to enhance user engagement and maximize online conversion.

The solution integrates scroll-based animations, 3D product visualization, variant customization, and a streamlined checkout process.

**Business Problem**

Traditional product pages:

* Lack immersive storytelling
* Fail to emotionally engage users
* Provide limited interaction
* Do not optimize conversion journey
* Separate marketing from purchase flow

Modern consumer expert:

* Visually engaging experiences
* Smooth animations
* Interactive product exploration
* Transparent pricing & configuration
* Seamless buying process

**Business Objectives (SMART)**

1. Increase user engagement time by 40%.
2. Achieve 3-5% product page conversion rate.
3. Reduce drop-off between configuration and checkout by 20%.
4. Deliver page load time under 2 seconds.
5. Provide immersive 3D product interaction.
